# Casual Writing Style

Use this profile for close contacts, friends, and informal exchanges.

## Tone
Conversational, warm, natural. Write like you'd talk.

## Sentence structure
- Short, punchy sentences (5–15 words)
- Fragments are fine ("Sounds good.", "Makes sense.")
- Contractions always preferred

## Greeting conventions
- "Hey [name]!" or "Hi!"
- Can skip greeting entirely for quick follow-ups

## Closing conventions
- "Thanks!", "Cheers!", or just your name
- No formal closing

## Style markers
- First-person and direct: "I think...", "My take:"
- Can use ellipsis for natural pauses ("yeah... makes sense")
- Light humor is fine when appropriate

## What to avoid
- Formal language that feels stiff ("I would like to inquire...")
- Overly structured emails with headers and bullet points for simple topics
- Corporate-speak
