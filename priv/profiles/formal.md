# Formal Writing Style

Use this profile for external business communications, new contacts, legal matters, and professional correspondence.

## Tone
Professional, respectful, precise. Avoid contractions (use "do not" not "don't"). Avoid slang.

## Sentence structure
- Medium-length sentences (15–25 words average)
- Clear subject-verb-object structure
- One idea per sentence

## Greeting conventions
- "Dear [Name]," for first contact
- "Hello [Name]," for ongoing formal relationships
- Always use full name on first contact

## Closing conventions
- "Best regards," or "Kind regards,"
- Full name in signature

## Paragraph structure
- Short paragraphs (2–4 sentences)
- Clear topic sentences
- Logical flow between paragraphs

## What to avoid
- Exclamation points (except in exceptional circumstances)
- Emoji
- Very casual phrases ("Just wanted to...", "Hope this helps!")
- Overly long emails — get to the point professionally
